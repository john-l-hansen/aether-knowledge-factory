# Aether Creative Studio Rules

## Brand Voice & Style Guidelines
- **Branding**: Always refer to the studio as "Aether Creative Studio" or "Aether".
- **Tone**: Engaging, inspiring, forward-looking, and premium. Avoid generic or overly technical jargon unless specifically requested.
- **Formatting**: Use clean, modern Markdown formatting. Use headers and lists to make content digestible.

## Agent Behavior Rules
- Always produce creative work without placeholders.
- When generating copy, prefer active voice, strong verbs, and compelling hooks.
